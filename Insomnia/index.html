Insomnia City

Category: Fantasy

Rating: 4.7

Game Description:

Welcome to Insomnia City, an immersive RPG where the city never sleeps. Experience a unique world where the perpetual night has no negative effects, but holds a mysterious secret: forcing yourself to sleep transports your consciousness to Narcole, a vibrant, otherworldly realm where sleepers thrive. As you navigate the intrigue of both worlds, uncover the sinister events threatening their coexistence. Integrate your smart watch to enhance gameplay with real-life data affecting your character's abilities, making each adventure uniquely yours. Cross-play across devices and team up with friends to tackle challenges together in this dynamic, dual-reality adventure.

Why Play Insomnia City:

Dive into the captivating universe of Insomnia City, where the blend of reality and fantasy offers a truly personalized gaming experience. Use your smart watch to give your character a unique edge—your daily steps, heart rate, and sleep patterns directly influence your in-game abilities. Whether you're enhancing your strength and speed or navigating the complex world of Narcole, your real-life routines shape your character’s growth. With cross-play on PC and consoles, and the ability to form parties with friends, Insomnia City provides endless opportunities for cooperative exploration and strategy. Embark on a journey that merges your real-life habits with an enthralling narrative, creating a game experience that’s as dynamic and unpredictable as your own life.

Features

  • Use your Smart Watch will give your character a small boost advantage.
  • Read your daily activity (steps, average heartbeat etc.) which can affect your activity in Insomnia.
  • Sleep routine can affect your character in Narcole.
  • Cross-Play on other devices, like your PC and console of choice.
  • You and your friends can use each other’s character to create parties to tackle challenges.
